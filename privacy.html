FLUID CASH

Privacy Policy

Your Data is Yours.

Fluid Cash does not collect, track, or sell your financial data. We believe your privacy is non-negotiable.

1. Zero-Server Architecture

We do not have a central database or servers for your transactions. All your financial records are stored exclusively on your devices and in your private Apple iCloud container.

2. On-Device AI

Our Vision AI features (bill scanning and OCR) process everything locally on your device. No images or extracted text are ever uploaded to any third-party cloud for processing.

3. iCloud Sync

Syncing between your devices and your family members is handled entirely by Apple's secure CloudKit infrastructure. We have no access to this data.

4. Third-Party Services

We use OneSignal purely to deliver push notifications (like bill reminders). These notifications do not contain sensitive data unless you explicitly enable it in settings.
