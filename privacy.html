Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

Your Privacy is Our Core Feature

Fluid Cash was built with a "Privacy by Design" philosophy. We believe your financial data is yours alone.

1. Zero Data Collection

We do not operate any backend servers. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal information, transaction history, or financial records. We have no way to access your data.

2. Secure iCloud Infrastructure

Fluid Cash uses Apple's CloudKit and iCloud Drive to sync your data across devices. Your data is encrypted and stored in your private iCloud container, accessible only by you (and your shared family members if you explicitly invite them).

3. On-Device Processing

  • Vision AI / OCR: When you scan a bill, the text recognition happens entirely on your device. No images or extracted text are ever sent to an external server.
  • Biometrics: If you enable FaceID/TouchID, the authentication is handled by the iOS Secure Enclave. The app never sees your biometric data.

4. Third-Party Services

We use OneSignal for delivering push notifications (such as bill reminders or family sync alerts). No financial amounts or sensitive titles are sent through OneSignal unless necessary for the notification to function, and no data is harvested for marketing purposes.
