Privacy Policy

Your Data, Your Control.

Fluid Cash is built on a "Zero-Footprint" philosophy. We do not have servers, we do not collect your financial data, and we certainly do not sell it.

iCloud Storage

All your transactions, settings, and bills are stored securely in your personal Apple iCloud container. We have no access to this data.

No Analytics

We do not track your behavior within the app. Your financial habits are your business, and they stay on your device.
